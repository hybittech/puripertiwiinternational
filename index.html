About Us

Building a Better Future Through Innovation

PT PURI PERTIWI INTERNATIONAL was established in 2022 in accordance with the Decree of the Ministry of Law and Human Rights AHU-0047374.ah.01.01 of 2022 concerning the Legalization of the Establishment of a Limited Liability Company Legal Entity.

We are engaged in various fields such as Trading, Agriculture, Farm, Production, and Software Application, committed to creating economic welfare and improving the quality of local industrial home products.
